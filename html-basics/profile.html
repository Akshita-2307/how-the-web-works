Gyaan ki Baatein:-

HTML
Let's us understand html with an analogy. Everyone have seen a car,but inorder for a car to function properly there are several steps.Cars aren't made magically.The first thing you need to do is to design the basic layout of you car,like you want it big or small or sleek whatever. That outer structure of your car is HTML.HTML provides the basic layout of a webpage. It's doesn't give you fancy logos or colours.It just builds a structure.
CSS
Now when our structure is ready, no one's going to buy it like that. You need to make it look eye pleasing and inorder to do that we need "colours". That's exactly what CSS does to our webpage,makes it standout.It designs our page and make it look every catchy and beautiful.All the different colours and designs you see on a webpage that comes from our beloved CSS,who fills colours into our black and white world.
JavaScript
Now again,will anyone buy a car which doen't function but looks pleasing?? That's a rhetorical question by the way.In order to make it work we need components,like gears,handels,brakes and many more.This is where our saviour "javascript comes into the picture". It provides all the basic functioning unit of our webpage including clicking,scrolling,summiting forms,typing and the list goes on and on.
